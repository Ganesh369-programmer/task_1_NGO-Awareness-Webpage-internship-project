Who We Are

Born from the belief that every individual holds the power to change a life, InAmigos Foundation is a community-driven platform igniting change across India.

🏛️

Who We Are

InAmigos Foundation is a registered NGO dedicated to uplifting marginalized communities through education, empowerment, and environmental action. We believe in people-led change — every volunteer, every rupee, every hour matters.

  • Community-driven approach
  • Transparent & accountable
  • Pan-India impact
🎯

Our Mission

To build an inclusive society where every child gets quality education, every woman discovers her strength, every animal is treated with compassion, and every community thrives sustainably.

  • Education for all children
  • Women empowerment programs
  • Eco-conscious initiatives
🌟

Our Vision

A world where inequality is history — where compassion drives governance, education fuels opportunity, and communities rise together regardless of caste, class, or gender.

  • Zero illiteracy by 2030
  • Empowered rural women
  • Green, sustainable India

Our Work

Six Pillars of Change

Each project is a promise — a commitment to act where action is needed most.

📚

BachpanShala

Bringing quality education to underprivileged children through free learning centres, digital literacy, and mentorship programs across rural India.

Education
✨

Udaan

Empowering women through skill training, entrepreneurship workshops, legal awareness drives, and self-help group formation to break cycles of dependency.

Empowerment
🐾

Jeev

Advocating for animal welfare through rescue operations, free veterinary camps, adoption drives, and awareness programs promoting compassion for all living beings.

Animal Welfare
🌱

Prakriti

Fighting climate change one tree at a time — plantation drives, clean-up campaigns, eco-education in schools, and sustainable living workshops.

Environment
❤️

Seva

Reaching the most vulnerable — distributing food, medical aid, clothes, and essentials to the elderly, homeless, and disaster-affected communities.

Community Support
🚀

Vikas

Equipping youth with 21st-century skills — coding, communication, financial literacy, and leadership development to fuel tomorrow's changemakers.

Skill Development

Our Journey

The Impact Timeline

From a small group of dreamers to a nationwide movement — our story in milestones.

2020

🌱 The Beginning

InAmigos Foundation registered and launched with a team of passionate volunteers and a dream to serve communities across India.

2021

🤝 Community Roots

First 500 volunteers onboarded. Community outreach programs launched in 3 states with food drives and healthcare camps.

2022

📚 BachpanShala Launch

Free education centres opened in 15 villages. Over 1,000 children enrolled in our learning programs within the first year.

2023

✨ Women Rise

Udaan program empowered 5,000+ women with skill training, financial literacy, and micro-enterprise support across 6 districts.

2024

🌿 Going Green

Prakriti initiative planted 100,000+ trees. Partnered with 50 schools for eco-education programs and waste management drives.

2025

🚀 Scaling Impact

Volunteer network crosses 2,000 active members. All six projects running simultaneously. Intern training program hits 30,000+.
